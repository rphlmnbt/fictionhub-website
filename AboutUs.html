FictionHub is a website that aims to give people access to the greatest and latest news about fiction. It offers a free service that provides the fiction lovers, be it Novels, Comics, Anime, and many more, their daily fix of news and updates. It started as a simple concept created by a Computer Engineering student as his project in Software Development. Through the years, it has managed to improve and gain a wide variety of users. Together, they have managed to make FictionHub a haven for writers and readers alike. FictionHub also enables its users to submit their reviews without the need to reveal their true identity. Truly, FictionHub is a reader's lounge.
